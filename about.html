About Havilah Forward Schools

Committed to Excellence in Education and Character Building Since Our Foundation

Our Mission

To lay a firm foundation upon which every child that goes through this institution would have an excellent academic pursuit. To train minds who would compete favorably and excel among equals wherever they find themselves. To leave a lifetime impact on any child that ever attends this institution.

Our Vision

To see our pupils and student bring glory to themselves and Nigeria. To be among the best 100 institutions in the world in neerer future. To be the leading educational institution that shapes future leaders through academic excellence, character development, and global competitiveness.

Our Story

Excellence Through Dedication

Havilah Forward Schools was founded with a clear vision: to provide world-class education that prepares students not just academically, but also morally and socially for the challenges of tomorrow.

Since our establishment, we have maintained an unwavering commitment to excellence. Our near 100% success rate in WAEC and NECO examinations is a testament to our dedication to quality education and our students' hard work.

What sets us apart is our holistic approach to education - we don't just teach subjects, we build character, instill values, and prepare our students to be responsible global citizens.
